I put the Tim in timeless photos.

Photography has been a passion of mine since I was a kid. It all started when I was ten years old and found my dad's old camera. From that moment on, I found myself falling in love with taking pictures. I was photographing almost everything I saw. Going into high school, I joined the yearbook team as a photographer. For the next four years, I developed my passion and skills by photographing various school events from dances to football games.

Now that I am attending university, I seek to use my experience to help those around the Bay Area in need of affordable photography assistance. As of today, I have professionally photographed portraits, parties, marathons, weddings, and more.

I would be happy to extend my skills and help capture the beautiful moments of your life.
